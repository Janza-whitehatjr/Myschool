Sabarigiri English School, Anchal is the first CBSE School in Kollam District. Since its inception in 1978-79, the school has made giant strides in the field of quality CBSE school education from kindergarten to Senior Secondary level which reflects on the worldwide network of alumni students holding prestigious posts and leading organizations in various fields since passing out. Being a boarding school, students are given homely care and every attempt is made to make them fine individuals useful to their family and the society around and the nation. The final slogan they carry from school is wherever you may be, let the word to be better on account of you. Our school works with the aspiration and commitment to provide an enjoyable and enriching educational experience to children. This temple of learning inspires and facilitates them to realise their full potential. The school emphasises academic excellence and all-round development of children by offering a fine blend of curricular and co-curricular activities and moral education.
